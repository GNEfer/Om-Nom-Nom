Authentic Taiwanese Food

Oyster Vermicelli

Oyster Vermicelli is a popular noodle soup in Taiwan. The main ingredients are oysters and Taiwanese vermicelli. The vermicelli used for this dish is made mostly with wheat flour and salt. It is often garnished with parsley or cilantro, and hot sauce is added by preference. The dish is commonly found in night markets and have different ingredients added, depending on the region of Taiwan.

Pineapple Cake

Pineapple cake is a popular pastry in Taiwan. It contains butter, egg, sugar, flour, and pineapple jam. It is excellent combination with tea and other drinks. It has a crumbly crust with a chewy pineapple filling.

Bubble Tea

Bubble tea is a very popular Taiwanese drink. There are two distinct types of bubble tea: Fruit-flavoured tea and Milk tea. There are a wide selection of toppings such as pearls or tapioca bubbles, grass jelly, pudding, and much more. Different choices of tea are commonly used, including black tea, green tea, and more.
