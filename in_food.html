Authentic Indian Food

Samosa

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry, generally filled with potatoes, peas, lentils, onions, spices, and sometimes meat such as ground beef, lamb, or chicken. They are deep fried to a golden brown colour in oil, and often served fresh with the richness of Indian chutney, sweet or savory. There are many variations of samosas in the different parts of India, each with their own uniqueness and taste with different fillings. The way they are folded and shaped vary as well.

Curry

Curry is a very popular dish in India. Although there are many other variations in other Asian countries, Indian style curry is one of the most renown. It uses many combinations of different spices or herbs, including chillies, often includes meat such as chicken, fish, and shellfish with many vegetables.

Most countries use curry powder, which is a spice mixture of different spices already pre-packaged, and saves the time of finding and buying individual spices to enjoy more of this delicious meal.

Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish made with spices, meat or vegetables, and rice. Most biryani is made with long grain rice, such as basmati, and lots of spices like nutmeg, mace, cloves, pepper coriander and much more. Biryani has two basic types: Pakki and Kacchi. In pakki, the cooked meat and rice are layered. However in kacchi, the raw meat and rice are layered before being cooked.
