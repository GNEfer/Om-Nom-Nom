Authentic Thai Food

Phad Thai

Phat Thai is a very popular stir fried rice noodle dish and can be commonly found at street vendors. Vegetables and meat combinations vary across the world, such as adding shrimp instead of chicken for example.

Tom Yum Goong

Tom Yum or Yam is a Thai spicy and sour soup. It is very popular around the world. It includes various vegetables and meat such as mushrooms, prawns, fish, and much more. It is characterized by the distinct hot and sour flavours with fragrant herbs used in the broth. The broth is made from lemongrass, lime juice, fish sauce, lime leaves, galangal (a plant in the ginger family), and crushed chilli peppers.

Green Curry

Green Curry is a central thai variety of curry. It uses green chillies, hence the color of the curry. Coconut milk and fresh green chilies are the base of this curry. Other ingredients are added such as meat, fish, fish sauce, green curry paste, palm sugar, thai eggplant, pea aubergine, and other vegetables, with the occasional fruit.
